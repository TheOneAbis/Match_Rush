My Plan: MATCH 3 PUZZLE GAME

Title: Match Rush

Game Info

Description: The objective of this game is to defeat your opponent (the AI).

This game is similar to games like Bejeweled. Both you and the AI have a base to protect, and the AI will begin sending troops to defeat your base.

You have a table full of various colored icons that you must move into rows/columns of 3, 4, or 5 that, when matched, will spawn your own troops to defend yourself.

Spawn enough troops to defeat the AI's base!!!

Genre: Puzzle

Platform: Desktop App

Story: Nothing really big here, mainly just this enemy guy wants to take over your country, so you gotta take his.

Graphic Style: 2D art with a similar aesthetic to that of "Puzzle Quest"

Gameplay: You and the AI have a base HP bar that goes down as troops get within range of them. Melee troops spawn from 3-of-a-kind moves, ranges troops spawn from 4-of-a-kind moves,
and massive slow-moving tanks spawn from 5-of-a-kind moves.
